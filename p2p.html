You can use this calculator to determine the potential returns on your P2P lending investments.
Here you have to enter the following details:
Investment Amount: The total amount you plan to invest in P2P lending.
Tenure: The duration for which you plan to lend your money.
Interest Rate: The expected annual return on your investment.
Facilitation Charge %: The percentage fee charged by the P2P platform for facilitating the lending.
Fees in €/₹/$: The fees charged by the platform in your preferred currency.
Recievable Interest: The interest amount you will receive after deducting the facilitation charges.
Total Payout: The total amount you will receive at the end of the tenure, including your initial investment and the receivable interest.
